Overview

Purpose

This personal portfolio site offers an in-depth look at Jaxon Gordon, showcasing both his professional trajectory and accomplishments. Visitors can explore Jaxon's comprehensive resume and diverse work experience through an intuitive interface designed with thoughtful navigation. Beyond his career achievements, the site provides insight into Jaxon's personal side through engaging sections dedicated to his various hobbies and interests that shape his life outside of work.

Audience

The intended audience for Jaxon Gordon's portfolio website include: potential employers, networking contacts, potential clients, friends, family, and acquaintances.

Dynamic elements

This site will be an interactive project gallery that includes filters and a search bar. The site will also work as a scrapbook showcasing many images of Jaxon and his family. Visitors will be able to interact with the images to look at them in more detail using an image zoom/magnify.

Home page

Professional Experience & Accomplishments

My diverse eight years in different industries has equipped me with confidence and a proven track record of achievement. My mechanical aptitude, developed during my time as a high school mechanic, provides a practical foundation. Working as a customer service representative refined my communication abilities, while my accounting internship taught me proper financial management practices. Beyond my work experience, I've earned certifications in Microsoft programs including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. I've also obtained specialized certifications in accounting and analytical software such as Drake Tax, IDEA, and Tableau.

Career Goals & Ambitions

I aim to leverage my extensive accounting and coding expertise to build a successful career as an accountant and controller. My long-term goal is to develop my capabilities further and become a valuable CFO or business owner driven by passion. As I continue to grow these talents and skills, I intend to contribute my time and abilities to non-profit organizations and my local community.

Interests & Hobbies

More About Me

I maintain a strong commitment to my personal interests and relationships. My wife and I enjoy exploring nature through hiking and international travel. Bird hunting in the cornfields of Idaho and Utah with my father and brothers provides meaningful family connection. As an avid sports enthusiast, I value attending MLB and NFL games. These personal pursuits drive my ambition for success in both my career and entrepreneurial ventures.
